DOCUMENT + AI PRIORITY
==================================================

The top AI priority is document workflows.

Design a system where uploaded student/application documents can:
- be classified by type
- be previewed/downloaded securely
- be processed by AI
- produce extracted data
- map extracted data into student/application fields

- passport -> name, surname, birth date, nationality, passport number, expiry date
- diploma/transcript -> school name, graduation year, grades if possible
